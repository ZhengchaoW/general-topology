MATH 8655: General Topology (Fall 2025)

Course Objectives

This graduate-level course introduces point-set topology. Topics include topological spaces, continuity, compactness, connectedness, countability and separation axioms, and an introduction to homotopy, etc.
See the course syllabus (PDF) here.

Textbook & References

Stephen Willard, General Topology.

James Munkres, A first course in Topology.
